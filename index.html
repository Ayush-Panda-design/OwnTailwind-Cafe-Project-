Our Story

Obsessed with
every variable.

We started Kōhī because we believed coffee deserved the same attention as fine wine. Every bean tells the story of its terroir — the altitude, the rainfall, the hands that picked it.

Our roastery sources directly from 14 origins. We roast in small batches every Thursday, so what arrives in your cup was roasted within 7 days. No exceptions.
